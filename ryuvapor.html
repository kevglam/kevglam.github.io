    RYU Apparel Vapor Collection Landing Page

    The Vapor Collection is RYU Apparel’s main high-intensity workout clothing collection. Built to move and keep your skin breathing while doing so. On one of the weeks of our summer campaign, we wanted to feature this collection and I was tasked to design a landing page for it. The page was meant to be a space where all of the Vapor items could live and where all of its features could be showcased to the consumer.

    Gray Oncology Solutions UI

    We had the opportunity to design for the startup company Gray Oncology Solutions. We were tasked with: designing parts of the user interface for their desktop app, refine their style guide and redesign their website. We learned a lot about designing for apps and got the chance to see a UI project come to life from conception to final product.

    "GrayOS is the only platform on the market that fully automates patient scheduling while being built from the ground up for oncology." gray-os.com

    This is an ongoing project and a collaboration with Maëlle Morin.

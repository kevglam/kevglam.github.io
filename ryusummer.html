    RYU Apparel Summer Campaign

    Sweat. Heat. Sand Dunes. Those were the keywords given to me when I was tasked with the project of conceptualizing and designing the 8-week 2022 Summer Campaign for RYU Apparel. Using our brand identity, I wanted to create material that captured the hot desert theme.

    The deliverables I was tasked to create are the following: Email Newsletters, Social Ads, Programmatic Ads, Social Posts, Social Reels, Website Headers and Copywriting for the content. (All copy in the content was written by me!)

    Email Newsletters
    For emails, I needed to spread our campaign through 8 weeks. Our general cadence is 1 to 2 emails per week. For this campaign I decided to go with 4 different topics (Summer Shorts, Wind Shells, Activewear and Summer Lifestyle) at 1 email per week. My strategy was to alternate between an email more focused on the overall theme of summer with a glimpse of product and then follow it with an email more focused on said product the following week.

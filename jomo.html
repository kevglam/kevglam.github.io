    JOMO Candle Co.

    JOMO is a local candle company based in Montreal with the goal of creating asian-inspired handcrafted candles. I was approached by them to work on many projects as their creative consultant, notably their packaging design and website design.

    They wanted something bright and pastel-esque. Minimal yet striking and light and airy. Below are the designs I made for their 8 oz Tins and their 4 oz Tins.

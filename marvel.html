    Audible Marvel's Wastelanders

    Audible was launching all 6 seasons of Marvel’s action packed episodic podcast across many of their international marketplaces and they’ve assembled us to save the day! Getting the opportunity to work on creative involving the superheroes who've inspired my younger self was an absolute dream.

    For this campaign, we conceptualized and delivered assets in all size formats and platforms to allow marketplaces the flexibility to post how they see fit.
    After establishing the foundational visual structure for the first season, we continued with the foundation we laid out while bringing in plot elements and look and feel of the newer seasons into the mix.

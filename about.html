    My name is Kevin G Lam (林德江)! Born in Montreal, Canada in 1996;

    I am a Graphic Designer and Videographer based in Montreal. Graduated from Concordia University in Computation Arts, topics that inspire my work revolve around Asian American Culture and Asian visual culture. I am interested in how my heritage ties into my work and world.

    I am Asian Canadian and growing up in such an amazing melting pot of culture definitely shines through in my work. I draw from my family culture (A splash of Chinese and Vietnamese), my hometown’s culture (A cup of Canadian) and the pop culture I grew up in (Hints of American, Japanese and Korean). Bake at 425°F for 23 years and voila! You got yourself a Kevin G Lam ready to create and inspire!

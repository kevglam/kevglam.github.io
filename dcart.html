    • Focal Points / Points Focaux Exhibition Posters (2019)

    Focal Points / Points Focaux is a year-end student art exhibition held at Concordia University under the Design and Computation Arts department. I had the opportunity to make promotional posters for the event. The poster features themes of focal points, intersections and the crossing point of the two featured departments (Design and Computation Arts).

    Mountain is a project that illustrates the Quantified Self in a subjective and emotional way.

    On the span of about 2 weeks, I have tracked data following a workout at the gym. After every work out. â€‹Each mountain represents my confidence levels and ego after a workout. The art piece is based on a subjective question I will be asking myself after every workout. The question is "How many mountains do you feel like you can lift?" (On a scale out of 7 mountains).

    My project is an emotional and subjective approach to the quantified self movement. Symbolically, each poster I created represents a mountain that I have conquered. The topographic maps that go with these mountains provide details of the day I conquer each mountain.

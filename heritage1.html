    • Heritage I (2016)

    ​​The goal of this video was to illustrate a newer generation of Asian culture. I wanted to create contrast between traditional Asian culture and today’s younger generation. In older times, Asian culture was very disconnected from one another. Nowadays, we have a big mixing of culture.

    "Heritage" is a video and multimedia series that I have created. It is an on-going project that centralizes on the theme of Asian culture. The series is, in a way, a self-portrait of myself but it is also a portrait of Asian Americans and Asian American culture. The project includes a video component as well as promotional components and 2D printed visuals.

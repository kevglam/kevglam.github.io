    RYU Apparel Bag Campaign Landing Page

    I was tasked with designing a landing page for RYU’s bag collection. One of the main priorities for this page was to give each bag its own space to present itself to the consumers. We wanted a page that served as a premium shopping experience. Somewhere to drive shoppers to and a place where they could quickly grasp the differences between each bag and discover which one best suit their needs.

    With RYU’s branding in mind, these are the desktop and mobile pages I came up with.
